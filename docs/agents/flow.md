# Flow

## Overview

Personal Marketing is a text-based RPG where **Anastacia** must complete a series of marketing jobs to save her struggling company.

## Starting Point

Anastacia begins the game at **Home** — a familiar, safe environment. From there she can explore the world, visit clients, and take on jobs.

## Core Loop

1. Anastacia travels to a **location** (a client's office, an event venue, a meeting room, etc.).
2. She interacts with **NPCs** (clients, competitors, colleagues) to receive or advance jobs.
3. She completes **jobs** — tasks tied to marketing deliverables.
4. Completing jobs earns progress toward saving her company.

## Win Condition

Anastacia saves her marketing company by completing enough jobs and building sufficient reputation before the company collapses.
